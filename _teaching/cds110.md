Introduces feedback control systems analysis and design in both time and frequency domains, emphasizing state space methods, robustness, and design tradeoffs. Topics include linear I/O systems, eigenvalue placement, LQR, output feedback with estimators, Bode/Nyquist frequency domain analysis, and effects of disturbances and unmodeled dynamics.
