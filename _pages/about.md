I am a PhD candidate in the department of Mechanical and Civil Engineering at the California Institute of Technology (Caltech), advised by Aaron D. Ames and Pietro Perona. 

I received my B.S. double major in Mechanical Engineering and Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) from UC Berkeley in 2023 and my M.S. in Mechanical Engineering from Caltech in 2025.

Research 
=========

My research interests lie in understanding the interplay between planning, control, learning, and search at different levels of the robot autonomy stack.
